# Top 20 Romantasy Tropes

A reference guide for popular romantasy (romantic fantasy) tropes to use when generating story concepts.

## The List

1. **Enemies to Lovers** - Antagonists who fall for each other through conflict and tension

2. **Fated Mates/Soulmates** - Destined to be together by magic, prophecy, or supernatural bond

3. **Forced Proximity** - Trapped together in a situation, feelings develop unavoidably

4. **Grumpy x Sunshine** - Brooding, cynical character paired with optimistic, cheerful character

5. **Morally Grey Love Interest** - Antihero with questionable ethics and complex motivations

6. **Chosen One** - Prophesied hero with special destiny, often reluctant

7. **Found Family** - Creating bonds stronger than blood through shared experience

8. **Magic Academy/Training Arc** - Learning powers while romance blooms in educational setting

9. **Forbidden Love** - Romance across enemy lines, different classes, or species barriers

10. **Touch Her and Die** - Overprotective, possessive love interest who threatens anyone who harms their partner

11. **Only One Bed** - Forced to share sleeping space, leading to intimacy and tension

12. **Fake Relationship** - Pretend dating/marriage that becomes real feelings

13. **Second Chance Romance** - Reunited after separation, past mistakes, or lost time

14. **Slow Burn** - Gradual relationship development with mounting sexual/romantic tension

15. **Power Imbalance** - Student/teacher, royal/commoner, captor/captive dynamics

16. **Sacrifice for Love** - Willing to give up power, immortality, duty, or destiny

17. **Secret Identity** - Hidden royal lineage, disguised magical being, or concealed past

18. **Rival Courts/Kingdoms** - Political intrigue, alliances, and betrayal meets romance

19. **Monstrous Love Interest** - Fae, vampire, dragon shifter, demon, or other supernatural being

20. **Bargain/Deal with Consequences** - Magical contract, debt, or deal that leads to romantic entanglement

## Popular Combinations

Some tropes work exceptionally well together:
- Enemies to Lovers + Forced Proximity
- Fated Mates + Forbidden Love
- Morally Grey LI + Touch Her and Die
- Magic Academy + Slow Burn
- Secret Identity + Fake Relationship
- Rival Courts + Political Marriage (Fake Relationship)
- Monstrous Love Interest + Sacrifice for Love
- Power Imbalance + Second Chance

## Sub-tropes & Variations

### Enemies to Lovers Variants:
- Academic rivals
- Warriors on opposite sides
- Political enemies
- Rival thieves/assassins

### Fated Mates Variants:
- Rejected mate bond
- Wrong mate/true mate conflict
- Mate bond that causes pain/conflict
- One-sided mate awareness

### Forced Proximity Variants:
- Stuck in magical realm
- Hiding from enemies together
- Quest/journey partnership
- Arranged marriage

## Notes for Story Generation

When combining tropes, consider:
- **Conflict sources**: What keeps them apart?
- **Stakes**: What happens if they fail/succeed?
- **Character arcs**: How does romance change them?
- **World-building**: What magical rules govern the romance?
- **Subversion opportunities**: How can you twist expectations?
